Helpers: things you can say
Invocation name is mood audio. Start commands with:
• Alexa, ask mood audio ...
Playback
  • “Alexa, ask mood audio what’s playing”
  • “Alexa, ask mood audio to play artist John Mayer” (most reliable)
  • “Alexa, ask mood audio to play album Continuum”
  • “Alexa, ask mood audio to play playlist Chill Mix”
  • “Alexa, ask mood audio to play songs by James Taylor”
  • “Alexa, ask mood audio to play John Mayer” (you can omit artist/album/playlist specifier; Alexa will still try to match)
Local control with “here”
When you add here, you are controlling moOde playback on this system directly. It does not mean “play on Echo speakers.”
  • “Alexa, ask mood audio to play John Mayer here”
  • “Alexa, ask mood audio to play artist Diana Krall here”
  • “Alexa, ask mood audio to play album Continuum here”
  • “Alexa, ask mood audio to play playlist Chill Mix here”
Vibe
Requires Last.fm Vibe to be enabled/configured on the Config page.
  • “Alexa, ask mood audio to vibe this song” (build/normalize vibe queue from current track context)
  • “Alexa, ask mood audio to vibe this song here” (same, but force play on moOde)
Controls
For transport, built-in commands work great:
  • “Alexa pause”
  • “Alexa resume”
  • “Alexa skip”
Skill-scoped alternatives:
  • “Alexa, ask mood audio to shuffle”
  • “Alexa, ask mood audio to repeat”
  • “Alexa, ask mood audio to pause”
  • “Alexa, ask mood audio to resume”
  • “Alexa, ask mood audio to next”
  • “Alexa, ask mood audio to thumbs up this track”
