controller now-playing anatomy

Purpose

This page documents the internal anatomy of now-playing/controller-now-playing.html.

It is a substantive controller surface with album-art/flip-card behavior, title/artist/album info, next-up bar, progress bar, integrated controls, podcast seek affordances, queue modal embedding, album modal workflows, personnel overlays, and embedded-vs-top-level navigation logic.

Main anatomical regions

  1. album art / art flip / favorite hotspot region
  2. title / artist / album / file-info text stack
  3. next-up bar
  4. progress bar
  5. integrated phone-style transport controls
  6. artist-details / personnel overlay region
  7. podcast modal family
  8. album modal family
  9. embedded queue modal family
  10. embedded/top-level back navigation logic

Album art / art flip / favorite hotspot region

Anchored by #album-art-wrapper, #album-art, #album-art-bg, #art-info-hotspot, #fav-heart, and #artBackText. This is the main visual now-playing region and one of the clearest places where art truth, personnel behavior, and phone-mode layout meet.

Title / artist / album / file-info text stack

Anchored by #track-title, #artist-link, #album-link, #file-info, #file-info-text, #mode-status, and #hires-badge.

Next-up bar

Anchored by #next-up, #next-up-img, #next-up-text, and #nextUpSpin. Clicking this region opens the queue modal by embedding controller-queue.html?embedded=1.

Progress bar

Anchored by #progress-bar-wrapper and #progress-fill. This is one of the clearest transport/progress regions and likely one of the first places where stream/podcast/local-file differences become visible.

Integrated phone-style transport controls

Anchored by #phone-controls, #activeCtlRing, #activeCtlSymbol, and the key buttons #btn-repeat, #btn-prev, #btn-play, #btn-next, #btn-shuffle, #btn-seek-back, #btn-seek-fwd, #btn-seek-back-inline, and #btn-seek-fwd-inline.

Artist-details / personnel overlay region

Anchored by #artist-details-container, #artist-details, and #personnel-info. This appears to handle personnel or artist-details overlay behavior, especially in phone mode.

Podcast modal family

Anchored by #modalOverlay, #modalThumb, #modalTitle, #modalSub, #modalHint, #epList, #modalCount, #modalRefresh, and #modalDeleteSelected.

Album modal family

Anchored by #npAlbumModal, #npAlbumArt, #npAlbumTitle, #npAlbumMeta, #npAlbumTracks, #npAlbumAppendBtn, #npAlbumCropBtn, and #npAlbumReplaceBtn.

This region bridges now-playing UI and queue-wizard behavior using /config/runtime, /config/diagnostics/playback, /config/queue-wizard/preview, and /config/queue-wizard/apply.

Embedded queue modal family

Anchored by #npQueueModal, #npQueueCloseBtn, and #npQueueFrame. It embeds controller-queue.html?embedded=1 and is the clearest structural bridge between the now-playing page and the fuller queue surface.

Embedded/top-level back navigation logic

Anchored by #cnpBack and explicit embedded=1 handling. In embedded mode, back/close behavior posts np-kiosk-hide-pane to the parent instead of navigating normally.

Practical “where do I start?” map

  • Art / flip behavior: start with #album-art-wrapper, #art-info-hotspot, and #fav-heart.
  • Text identity stack: start with #track-title, #artist-link, #album-link, and #file-info.
  • Next-up bar: start with #next-up and the queue modal opener logic.
  • Progress / controls: start with #progress-bar-wrapper and #phone-controls.
  • Album queue actions: start with #npAlbumModal and the append/crop/replace button logic.
  • Queue opening behavior: start with #npQueueModal and #npQueueFrame.

Current status

This is the third real page-anatomy reference in the wiki. Its main job is making explicit that controller-now-playing.html is not just “the now-playing page,” but a multi-region controller surface.
